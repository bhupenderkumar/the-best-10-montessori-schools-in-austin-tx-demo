Our Mission & History

THE BEST 10 Montessori Schools in AUSTIN, TX, was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide a high-quality Montessori education to the children of Austin. Our school is fully accredited by the Association Montessori Internationale (AMI) and is committed to upholding the principles of Dr. Maria Montessori.

Our teaching approach is centered around the child, with a focus on hands-on learning, individualized instruction, and collaborative play. Our experienced teachers create a supportive environment that encourages children to take risks, explore their interests, and develop a love of learning.

We believe that education is not just about academics, but also about character development, social skills, and emotional intelligence. Our extracurricular programs, including sports, music, and art, provide opportunities for children to develop their passions and interests outside of the classroom.
