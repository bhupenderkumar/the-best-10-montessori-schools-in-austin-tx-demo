Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5, with a focus on social, emotional, and academic development. Our experienced teachers provide a nurturing environment that fosters a love of learning and prepares children for success in kindergarten and beyond.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves children from Kindergarten to 4th grade, with a focus on hands-on learning, individualized instruction, and collaborative play. Our experienced teachers create a supportive environment that encourages children to explore their interests and develop a love of learning.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program serves children from 5th to 8th grade, with a focus on academic rigor, critical thinking, and social responsibility. Our experienced teachers provide a supportive environment that encourages children to take risks, explore their interests, and develop a love of learning.
